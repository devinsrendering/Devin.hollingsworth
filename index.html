About

My name is Devin Hollingsworth, and I have a lifelong passion for technology and how things work. From an early age, I spent much of my free time tinkering—modding phones and game consoles, building PCs, modifying games, and creating small projects using salvaged parts and platforms like Raspberry Pi.During high school, I further developed this interest through vocational coursework in residential and industrial electrical engineering, which gave me a strong technical foundation and hands-on problem-solving experience. While this work sharpened my skills, it also pushed me to seek a deeper understanding—specifically the ability to create systems and products from the ground up.That curiosity led me into software development. I am now actively learning and working in programming, web and app development, and game development, and each step continues to open new creative and technical opportunities. I enjoy building functional, well-designed solutions and continuously expanding my skill set. My goal is to build a long-term career in technology, with the eventual aim of becoming independent and launching my own business where I can design, develop, and sell my own products and services.
